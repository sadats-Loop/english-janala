Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?

You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.

2. Is this website free to use?

Yes, our basic learning materials, interactive lessons, and vocabulary builders are completely free to use for all learners.

3. Do I need to create an account?

Creating an account is optional for browsing lessons, but signing up helps you save your progress and track your learning milestones.

4. How can I build my English vocabulary?

You can use our interactive vocabulary builder sections categorized by levels, complete with meanings, pronunciations, examples, and synonyms.

5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

Yes, once you successfully complete our structured learning paths and final quizzes, certificates of completion will be made available.
